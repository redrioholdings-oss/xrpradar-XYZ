⚠️ Common Misconceptions — Set the Record Straight
❌ MYTH
"Ripple controls XRP"
✅ REALITY
XRP runs on the XRPL, which is decentralised and maintained by the independent XRPL Foundation. Ripple holds XRP but cannot create, destroy, or freeze it.
❌ MYTH
"Ripple can print more XRP"
✅ REALITY
XRP has a fixed maximum supply of 100 billion — hardcoded into the protocol. No mining, no inflation, no new XRP can ever be created. Supply only decreases as tiny amounts are burned per transaction.
❌ MYTH
"XRP is a security"
✅ REALITY
Judge Torres ruled in 2023 that XRP is NOT a security in programmatic sales. The SEC settled with Ripple in 2025. XRP now operates with full US regulatory clarity for the first time.

XRP is no longer knocking on the door of traditional finance — it's building new springboards for growth and utilization.
